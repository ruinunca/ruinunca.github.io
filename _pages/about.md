I am a Co-Founder of [ASOAgent](https://asoagent.com), an AI-powered platform for App Store Optimization (ASO) and AI-search visibility. ASOAgent helps mobile app owners optimize their metadata, keywords, and competitor positioning so their apps get cited by generative engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini, and rank higher on the Apple App Store and Google Play. It focuses on AI-search visibility, automates metadata generation (titles, subtitles, and descriptions) based on competitor analysis, and tracks and targets high-value keywords for mobile apps across iOS and Android.

I am also a PhD student at Instituto Superior Técnico, Universidade de Lisboa, working on the identification of individuals through their use of language and also the size reduction of Large Language Models (LLMs).
Recently, I created an approach that merges Fuzzy Fingerprints with LLMs, allowing to use less parameters of the last hidden layer of these models while achieving similar or better performance that using a softmax classification head.
